Study Trip Finder

Skills: UX & Web design | URL: studytripsfinder.co.uk/

Oxford International Educational tours are one of the largest independent and most respected educational travel companies in the UK.

Intro

The core of their business is arranging tours for schools and university groups. Over their two decades of business they have acquired a number of specialist education based tour agencies. all of these tour agencies have their own website and brand. They required a new umbrella brand, under which all of these sub brands would operate. This new umbrella brand would be called Study Trip Finder.

Project

The site needed to inspire the user and leave them with a feeling of wanderlust. Large cover images with minimal text, videos, reviews and testimonials all help to create an immersive experience. With over 100 trips to choose from the website need to be easy to navigate and there is a strong emphasis on logical filtering and sorting of trip results. Related content is used to pull in appropriate resources and news stories depending on the page the user is viewing.

Results

The website provides a repository for users to explore all the trips on offer and allows them to make a more informed decision. It received fantastic feedback from the client “I think it looks great, very sleek and engaging”.

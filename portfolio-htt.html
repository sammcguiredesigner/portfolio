Hull Truck Theatre

Skills: UX & UI | URL: Aristotheme

A pioneering theatre with a unique, powerful, contemporary Northern Voice, locally rooted and national in reach, inspiring artists, exciting audiences and supporting communities to reach their greatest potential.

Intro

Hull Truck Theatre required an overhaul of their website to compliment a re-brand and new show artwork styling. It was essential that the website delivered simple intuitive UX, a strong brand presence and highlighted each of the performance’s personalities.

Project

The site optimises customisation, creativity and originality through responsive web design using Umbraco open source CMS. We incorporated a cutting-edge Spektrix ticketing system, which helps maximise revenue. We explored the essence and personality of each show, and then used full-bleed large scale images with minimal text to bring that personality to life. Responsive web design means that the user won’t get a lesser experience just because they’re using a smaller device. From watching trailers to booking tickets, everything’s clear and accessible, no matter what device they’re using. A better user experience means happier customers and time saved at the box office. Features of the ticketing system such as best seat in the house, stored payment details and automatic renewal of memberships, give users the best possible booking experience.

Results

Since the website has gone live they have seen a massive increase in engagement, page views have doubled, box office queries have been reduced and the site is now their strongest sales channel, selling 43% of all their tickets. Prior to the new website 36% of their tickets were sold online. The new website and creative work helped contributed towards Hull gaining the title of City of Culture 2017.

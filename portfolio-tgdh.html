The Graphic Design House

Skills: Branding, Graphic Design, Website | URL: tgdh.co.uk

A creative full service agency, offering digital design, print design, branding and strategy.

Intro

The Graphic Design House began as a traditional print based design agency. As the web and digital side of the business increased, they required a full rebrand and rework of all their online and offline collateral, to reflect their new business model.

Website

It was decided that the projects should be the main focus of the website. With this in mind we approached the website in the same way an art gallery might display their work. By distilling the brand down to itâ€™s essential elements the work is given maximum visual impact, with no interference from the branding. By using only black and white and two classic typefaces, the branding is strong and simple. The website uses strong imagary, parallax and micro interactions to bring each case study to life.

Business cards

The business cards reflected the concept of the brand and website. In the same way that each project has a unique story, every member of staff has a unique personality. The design allows each staff member to pick their own image based on a hobby or interest. This has proven to be a great conversation starter and always leaves a lasting impression with clients.

Email boilerplate

An updated email template was required to reflect the new look and feel for the business. This was taken one step further. An email boilerplate was developed making use of all the most up to date methods in html email. The idea behind the boilerplate was to create a generic html email, which was based on tried and tested html markup and used all the necessary bug fixes and hacks (Client specific styles, Resets, Content styles, Mobile style, IOS fixes, Android fixes, MSO fixes & Google font set and fixes). The boilerplate contains a serious of elements and grids, which are commonly used in email design and development, all of which were tested fully in Litmus. The result has meant a dramatic reduction in email development time, meaning templates and custom static emails can be produced in a fraction of the time it has taken previously.

Results

The overall brand and website has receive fantastic praise. It has elevated the agency to one that people can take seriously and trust.

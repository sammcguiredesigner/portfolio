Watford Palace Theatre

Skills: UX & Web design | URL: watfordpalacetheatre.co.uk/

Watford Palace Theatre is a 600-seat theatre, serving a diverse local community.

Intro

They required creative web design and a intuitive user experience, which would encourage online bookings and return visits.

Website

The result is a clean, contemporary layout, reflecting Watford Palace Theatre’s modern, exciting approach, while having plenty of scope for capturing the individual personality of each production.

By using Umbraco ‘Page components’, we were able to give Watford Palace’s content editor the freedom to turn every page into a rich, immersive experience. The design needed this flexibility in order to reflect the creative range of performances, workshops and community events.

Results

The website has been a tremendous success, boosting revenue and building brand identity for Watford Palace theatre. This has led to an overall increase in bookings, while also easing bookings and enquiries at the box office through encouraging audiences to go online. Online donations have doubled since going live, adding a vital source of revenue for Watford Palace Theatre.

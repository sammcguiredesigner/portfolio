About

I am Sam McGuire, a Senior Web Designer based in Southsea, UK.

I have over 12 years experience working with a broad spectrum of clients from small start ups to international brands, conceptualising and developing visual solutions for a range of digital and offline projects.

I have a keen eye for detail and a passion for well thought out, creative design. A multidisciplinary background enables me to take on a wide variety of projects with a creative approach.
